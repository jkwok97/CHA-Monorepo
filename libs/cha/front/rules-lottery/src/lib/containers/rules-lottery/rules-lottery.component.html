Draft Lottery
  • The bottom 9 teams in the regular season standings qualify for the draft lottery (the number of team eligible is base on the percentage of total NHL teams eliglbe for the NHL draft lottery)
  • All eligible teams qualify for the 1st overal selection
  • No team can worsen their draft position by more than one spot
  • Odds of improving one's draft position are based upon final regular season positioning. Odds of obtaining the 1st overall selection are as follows:
    • 24th - 36%
    • 23th - 23%
    • 22th - 13%
    • 21th - 9%
    • 20th - 7%
    • 19th - 5%
    • 18th - 4%
    • 17th - 3%
