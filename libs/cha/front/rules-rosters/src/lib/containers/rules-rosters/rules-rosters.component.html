Roster Rules
  • CHA rosters may comprise of a maximum of 30 skaters, including at least 2 (but no more than 3) goaltenders (see Emergency Goaltender Rule).
  • CHA rosters must have a minimum of 24 players (including goaltenders)
  • Roster maximum and salary cap only apply during the regular season
  • Rosters must be in compliance of the salary cap before each set of regular season games. If this is breached, the league will perform the necessary transactions by proxy
